# CKF Sistema Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose
CKF Sistema is the internal quotation system for CKF Manutencao. It replaces manual Word and spreadsheet budget creation with a fast, reliable, auditable web app for creating, editing, viewing, duplicating, deleting, filtering, and exporting company quotations.

## Users
Primary users are CKF Manutencao employees creating real client/service budgets during daily operations. They need to finish a quotation in under two minutes with minimal training. Secondary users are administrators or responsible staff reviewing history, status, creator, creation date, and update date.

## Success
The system is successful when staff stop using Word for quotations, values are calculated consistently, old quotations are easy to find, and exported PDF/XLSX files look like official CKF Manutencao documents.

## Brand And Tone
Professional, direct, solid, operational. The interface should feel like a quiet internal operations console, not a marketing site. The official document output is the visual anchor: black header, white paper, compact table, exact company data.
